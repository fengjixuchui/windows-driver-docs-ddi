## -description


Used to locate the WPP log
for WER reporting.

Offset of DriverContext into WPP_AUTOLOG_HEADER.

Offset of Size field into WPP_AUTOLOG_HEADER.


### -field WppSizeOfAutoLogHeaderSizeField

 Size of Header size field.
