# _DXGK_VGPU_CAPABILITY structure


## -description

Contains info about a virtual GPU capability.

## -struct-fields

### -field MinValue

When the vGPU is running, each capability cannot be lower than *MinValue* and more than *MaxValue*.

### -field MaxValue

When the vGPU is running, each capability cannot be lower than *MinValue* and more than *MaxValue*.
