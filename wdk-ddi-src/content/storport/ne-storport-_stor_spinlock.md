# _STOR_SPINLOCK enumeration


## -description


The STOR_SPINLOCK enumeration is used to specify the type of a spinlock.


## -enum-fields




### -field DpcLock

Indicates a DPC spinlock. 


### -field StartIoLock

Indicates a StartIo spinlock.  


### -field InterruptLock

Indicates an Interrupt spinlock.
