# IPartThumbnail::SetThumbnailContent


## -description


The <b>SetThumbnailContent</b> method sets the thumbnail content for the part.


## -parameters




### -param pContentType






#### - contentType [in]

The type of content for the thumbnail.


## -returns



<b>SetThumbnailContent</b> returns an <b>HRESULT</b> value.
