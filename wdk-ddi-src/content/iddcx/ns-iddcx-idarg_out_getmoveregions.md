# IDARG_OUT_GETMOVEREGIONS structure


## -description

Gives information about move regions that were handled by the OS.

## -struct-fields

### -field MoveRegionOutCount

                     [out] Number of move regions the OS copied into the <b>pMoveRegions</b> array.
