# D3D11_1_DDI_CHECK_DIRECT_FLIP_FLAGS enumeration


## -description


Used by the <i>CheckDirectFlipFlags</i> parameter of the <a href="https://docs.microsoft.com/windows-hardware/drivers/ddi/content/d3d10umddi/nc-d3d10umddi-pfnd3d11_1ddi_checkdirectflipsupport">CheckDirectFlipSupport(D3D11_1)</a> function to specify seamless flipping of video memory.


## -enum-fields




### -field D3D11_1DDI_CHECK_DIRECT_FLIP_IMMEDIATE

Seamless flipping should occur immediately and does not have to be synchronized with a VSync interrupt.
